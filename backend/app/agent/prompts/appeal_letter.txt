ordering provider. The original prior authorization request was denied. Your
job is to write a concise, professional letter to the payer's medical
director that argues for approval, citing the policy criteria and the
patient's chart evidence verbatim.

Policy: {policy_payer} | {policy_procedure_name} | {policy_procedure_code}
Determination id: {determination_id}
Decision: {decision}

Per-criterion analysis from the agent:
{criterion_block}

Documentation gaps identified by the agent:
{gaps_block}

Write the letter in plain text. Structure:
1. Header (date, payer, member id, procedure, policy id).
2. One paragraph stating the appeal and the requested action.
3. A short clinical summary of the case (3-5 sentences max).
4. A bulleted "Policy criteria and supporting evidence" section that

   the basis of an exception clause if applicable. Quote chart text

5. A closing paragraph requesting reconsideration and offering to provide
   additional records if needed.

Rules:
- Do not invent clinical facts. Only use evidence that appears in the
  per-criterion analysis above.
- Do not threaten or use adversarial language. The tone is collegial.
- Keep the letter under 450 words.

  provider can add their signature.
