Bariatric Surgery (Roux-en-Y Gastric Bypass, Sleeve Gastrectomy)
CPT 43644, 43775
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

COVERAGE RATIONALE

Bariatric surgery is medically necessary when ALL of the following criteria
are met:

1. The member is at least 18 years of age and has a body mass index (BMI) of
   either:
   a. 40 kg/m^2 or greater, OR
   b. 35 kg/m^2 or greater with at least one obesity-related comorbidity,
      such as type 2 diabetes mellitus, obstructive sleep apnea, hypertension,
      or non-alcoholic steatohepatitis.

2. The member has documented participation in a physician-supervised weight
   management program of at least six (6) consecutive months duration within
   the two (2) years preceding the prior authorization request, with monthly
   visits documented in the medical record.

3. The member has completed a psychological evaluation by a licensed mental
   health provider that confirms the absence of contraindications to surgery
   and demonstrates an understanding of postoperative requirements.

4. The member has completed a nutritional evaluation by a registered
   dietitian and demonstrates understanding of the postoperative diet.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

Bariatric surgery is NOT covered when any of the following applies:

A. The member has an untreated major depressive disorder, active substance
   use disorder, or active eating disorder.

B. The member is pregnant or planning pregnancy within the next twelve (12)
   months.

C. The member has a medical condition that contraindicates general
   anesthesia.

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

The following must be submitted with the prior authorization request:

i. Documentation of height and weight measurements at each monthly visit
   during the supervised weight management program.

ii. The full psychological evaluation report, including the licensed
    provider's recommendation.

iii. The nutritional evaluation report.

iv. A list of obesity-related comorbidities with supporting clinical
    documentation.
